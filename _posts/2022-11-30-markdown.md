## Emphasis

### **Bold**

```html
**bold**
__bold__
```
- **bold**   
- __bold__

### *Italic*

```html
*Italic*
_Italic_
```
- *Italic*
- _Italic_

### ***Bold and Italic***

```html
***Bold and Italic***
___Bold and Italic___
__*Bold and Italic*__
**_Bold and Italic_**
```
- ***Bold and Italic***   
- ___Bold and Italic___   
- __*Bold and Italic*__   
- **_Bold and Italic_**

## Blockquotes

### One Pharagraphs
```html
> Hello World!
```
> Hello World!   

### Multiple Pharagraphs

```html
> Hello World!
> 
> Hello World!
```
> Hello World!
> 
> Hello World!

### Nested Blockquotes
```html
> Hello World!
> 
>> Hello World!
```
> Hello World!
> 
>> Hello World!

### Blockquotes with Other Elements
```html
> ### Hello World!
> 
> - Hello World!
>
>> *Hello* **World**
```
> #### Hello World!
> 
> - Hello World!
>
>> *Hello* **World**
